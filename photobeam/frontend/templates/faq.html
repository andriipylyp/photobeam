What is {{settings.app_name}}?
{{settings.app_name}} is the ultimate solution for sharing and growing event memories effortlessly! Our platform allows event hosts and guests to create, access, and contribute to shared photo albums in real time.
How does it work?
  1. Host Setup: Event organizers create a cloud album for their special day—whether it’s a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or any celebration.
  2. Guest Access: Guests scan a unique QR code provided at the event to instantly join the album.

  • Live Updates: View and upload memories in real time as the event unfolds.
