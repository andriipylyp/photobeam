How It Works

  • Host Setup: Event organizers create a cloud album for their special day—whether it’s a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or any celebration.
  • Guest Access: Guests scan a unique QR code provided at the event to instantly join the album.
  • Real-Time Contributions: Using their phones, guests can upload photos directly to the album, adding their unique perspective to the event's story.

Key Features

  • Easy Access: No downloads or complicated sign-ups required—just scan and share!
  • Live Updates: View and upload memories in real time as the event unfolds.
  • Secure & Private: Album access is restricted to those with the QR code, ensuring privacy.
  • Download Anytime: Hosts can download the complete album after the event to relive the moments.
